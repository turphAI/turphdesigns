Refined Color Strategy: Neutral + Accents

All palettes use #f8fafb neutral backgrounds with pastel colors as borders, indicators, and highlight blocks. Choose based on which accent colors best align with your thought leadership brand:

Why This Approach Works:

• Professional: Clean neutral backgrounds let your content shine
• Sophisticated: Subtle accents avoid visual overwhelm
• Scalable: Easy to add new sections without color conflicts
• Accessible: High contrast text on neutral backgrounds
• Authoritative: Perfect for thought leadership content
